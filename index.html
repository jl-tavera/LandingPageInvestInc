About

It's all about openness

Investors traditionally have funded Startups the same way since the 1980s: go to networking events, do the due diligence process and hopefully, get a nice return in about 3 to 5 years time. With the rise of the internet, the Startup ecosystem saw a new revival in ways of getting funded. Cryptocurrency, Rolling Funds, Crowdfunding, Tokens, etc, all these tools are at the disposal for founders to use. However, not many of them are used in traditional processes.

At Navel, we seek to change that by creating an open-ended community where founders and investors can craft together solutions that fit their needs. This way, we believe we help democratize access to capital for everyone.
