About

It's all about openness

Startups traditionally have been getting funded the same way since the 1980s: go to networking events, follow the investors requirements and hopefully, get funded in about 6 to 12 months of starting the process.

At Navel, we seek to change that by creating an open-ended community where founders and investors can craft together solutions o that fit their needs. This way, we believe we help democratize access to capital for everyone.
